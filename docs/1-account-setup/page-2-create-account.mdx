# Create Your Instructor Account

It's time to get your account setup! the feel of Typist! We'll do this by trying a few timings and then looking at our keyboarding profile!

:::info

Before you can continue, you'll need Typist's Instructor Key in order to create your account. The Instructor Key is likely inside the email that brought you to this page.

If you don't yet have it, please reach out to your Course Coordinator or Department Chair.

:::

## Create Your Account

1. Head on over to [Typist](https://www.typistapp.ca) @ www.typistapp.ca.
2. Click 'Create Account'.
3. Fill out the forms and you're done!
