## General Tips

- Navigate to the next step using the 'Next' link at the bottom of each page.
- Video speed can be increased or decreased. Click 'Play', and then click '1x' in the bottom right of the video to change playback speed.
- To more easily see content in the videos, use full screen mode (bottom right corner of each video)!
- Complete the activities as you work along; don't leave until the end.
- Add reactions as you watch the videos!
- Any of the videos on this site may be shared with your students. Simply click the link icon at the top of each video to copy the video link!
